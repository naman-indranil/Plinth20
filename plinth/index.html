Techno-Management Fest

Plinth 2020

Techno-Management Fest, Jaipur

About Us... Plinth is the annual inter college Techno-Management-Literary Festival of The LNM Institute of Information Technology, Jaipur. Plinth proudly encompasses and transcends the boundaries of science, technology, management,and literature, providing its audience with a completely indelible experience. Plinth extravagantly puts its efforts in providing a ‘platform’ to individuals to exhibit their talents and acumen in various activities designed to grill the very best. In Plinth, we see all kinds of things. From the craziest innovation, to the simplest of things which can be highly consequential but we might not have gazed our eye on. We aim to tap inside each and everyone present and help refine the diamonds inside all.
